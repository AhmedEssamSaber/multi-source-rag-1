# docs/mass_effect.txt

Title: Mass Effect

Definition:
Mass effect means pressure exerted by a lesion on adjacent brain structures.

Common Causes:

* Tumor
* Hemorrhage
* Edema
* Abscess
* Large infarction

Imaging Findings:

* Compression of nearby brain tissue
* Ventricular compression
* Sulcal effacement
* Midline shift
* Herniation risk in severe cases

Clinical Symptoms:

* Headache
* Vomiting
* Focal neurological deficits
* Reduced consciousness
* Seizures

Severity:
Ranges from mild displacement to life-threatening herniation.

Management:
Treatment depends on cause and urgency.
Examples include surgery, steroids, drainage, or critical care support.
